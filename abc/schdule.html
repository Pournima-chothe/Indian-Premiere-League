The BCCI announced the schedule for the first 21
matches which will be held across 17 days.

The much-awaited schedule for the Indian Premier League 2024 was finally announced on Thursday,February 22 by BCCI(Board of Control for cricket in India).Defending Champions Chennai Super Kings will start their title defence by taking on Royal Challengers Bangalore in the curtain raiser on Friday,March 22 at MA Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai. After the opening encounter fans will get to witness double headersover the action packed weekend where Punjab Kings will take on Delhi Capitals in the second game on march 23 in Mohali followed by Kolkata Knight Riders and Sunrisers Hyderabad locking horns in the evening at Eden Gardens Kolkata on the same day. The first mega Sunday of the season will further treat fans with two riveting encounters as Rajasthan Royals and Lucknow Super Giants willface off in Match 4 at Sawai Mansingh Stadium,Jaipur.
Later on in the evening, fans will get to witness five-time champions Mumbai Indians going up against Gujarat Titans at Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmednagar. Earlier, it was expected that the tournament might be moved out of the country due to the elections.However, the BCCI have tried their level best to host the tournament in India to allow
fans to witness their level best to host the tournament teams in their favourite teams in their respective homegrounds.
